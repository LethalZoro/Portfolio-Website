# PRODUCT.md

register: brand

## Product purpose

Personal portfolio for Muhammad Mustafa, an AI engineer (LLM agents, voice agents, computer vision) with a hardware spine (FPGA, GNN hardware-Trojan detection, embedded). The site is the proof of craft: a hiring manager or founder opens the link, skims for 40 seconds, and must leave with one memorable image and instant confidence in engineering depth.

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, startup founders, fellow engineers. Desktop-first impression, heavy mobile share from LinkedIn taps.

## Brand voice

Precise, luminous, quietly audacious. "Cosmic AI lab": deep violet-black space, electric violet + cyan signal light, instrumentation details in monospace. Confident engineer, not sci-fi cosplay.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing kit (centered stack, icon cards, gradient text).
- Matrix/hacker green terminal cosplay.
- Beige minimalist template with one timid accent.
- The editorial display-serif + italic + mono-label magazine lane.

## Strategic principles

- Dark-first, with a genuinely designed light mode. System preference respected, toggle persisted.
- One signature moment: the interactive neural particle field in the hero. Everything else supports it.
- All content is data-driven from `data/*.ts`; adding or reordering a project touches zero components.
- Motion is orchestrated and ≤400ms; `prefers-reduced-motion` fully honored.
